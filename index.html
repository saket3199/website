About us

MEGALEIO is a technical event organized by
St.John College of Engineering and Management ( SJCEM ), Palghar.

The Event is planned and organized by student body of SJCEM.
"The basic idea of organizing this event is to provide a platform to the students at state and National level to showcase
their talent and provide information on latest technologies in Engineering".

The Event is inspired by owerwhelming response of the student of SJCEM during intra college technical festival,science fest
(Annual Technical Exhibition).Annual project will showcase the participation of the students in multiple national level competions.
We are hopeful that the initiative will bring a new era of
"Learning by Sharing" in vicinity of campus.
Students from other colleges are being invited to participate in the events.
